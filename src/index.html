Small Modal

The small modal is perfect for short confirmation dialogs or very compact interfaces with few elements.

Medium Modal

The medium modal is large enough to fit a two column layout where both columns will have a readable line-length on most desktop devices.

Single column layouts will still be within a readable line length on the medium size.

Large modal

This modal will be fullscreen until it is as wide as the calcite-web grid's max-width. This enables you to use gridded layouts within the modal and keeps your modal from growing beyond a readable line length.

Fullscreen modal

This modal will always be fullscreen. Use in situations where the elements contained inside your modal need the full screen realestate (maps, etc)
